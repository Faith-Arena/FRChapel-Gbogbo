About Full Redemption Chapel, Gbogbo

Full Redemption Chapel, Gbogbo (Faith Arena) is a vibrant branch of the Jesus is Lord Foundation International. We're committed to building a community rooted in Christ, reaching souls with the full gospel, and maturing believers through sound teaching and spiritual growth.

About Our General Overseer

Rev. Dr. Francis Olonade is the esteemed General Overseer of Jesus is Lord Foundation International. Called into ministry in 1980, he is a passionate evangelist, teacher, and spiritual father to many. Through his dedication to the Word and global outreach, he continues to lead a powerful gospel movement across nations.

He is supported by his wife, Rev. Dr. Grace Olonade, who serves as Vice President and Senior Pastor. She is a gifted minister and teacher of the Word, working side-by-side with her husband to mentor ministers, teach the Bible, and advance the Kingdom of God globally.

About Our Resident Pastor

Pastor (Mrs) Olawale is the Branch Pastor of Full Redemption Chapel, Gbogbo. She currently serves as the Principal Assistant Registrar at Obafemi Awolowo University. With a background in administration and ministry, she is a passionate, spirit-filled leader focused on spiritual maturity and excellence in service.
