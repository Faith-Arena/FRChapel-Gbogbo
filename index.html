About Our General Overseer

Rev. Dr. Francis Olonade is the esteemed General Overseer of Jesus is Lord Foundation International. He was called into ministry in 1980 and has since been a relentless voice of evangelism and revival across nations. Known for his powerful teaching and healing ministry, he has impacted countless lives with the Gospel. His ministry emphasizes holiness, spiritual growth, and equipping believers for end-time harvest.

He is supported by his wife, Rev. Dr. Grace Olonade, who serves as Vice President and Senior Pastor. She is a respected minister, counselor, and teacher of the Word. Together, they lead with integrity, vision, and deep spiritual insight, overseeing several branches globally and mentoring pastors and leaders across denominations.

About Our Resident Pastor

Pastor (Mrs) Olawale is the Branch Pastor of Full Redemption Chapel, Gbogbo. She currently serves as the Principal Assistant Registrar at Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife. With a strong background in administration and spiritual leadership, she has been instrumental in the development and spiritual growth of the church.

Pastor Olawale is passionate about empowering others through teaching, mentoring, and example. Her leadership is marked by excellence, dedication to sound doctrine, and a heart for building a Christ-centered community.
