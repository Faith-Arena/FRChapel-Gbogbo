About Full Redemption Chapel, Gbogbo

Full Redemption Chapel, Gbogbo (Faith Arena) is a vibrant and spiritually empowering church in Modakeke, Osun State, Nigeria. We are part of the global movement of Jesus is Lord Foundation International, committed to sharing the Gospel and making disciples of Jesus Christ. Our church is a place where the Word of God is preached in its purest form, and individuals are nurtured in faith. We strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone can grow in their relationship with Christ and be part of a supportive community. Our mission is to raise mature disciples and impact lives positively, not just locally but globally.

General Overseers

Rev. Dr. Francis Olonade is the General Overseer of Jesus is Lord Foundation International. Called and commissioned in 1980, he continues to lead a global evangelism movement. His wife, Rev. Dr. Grace Olonade, serves as Vice President and Senior Pastor of Full Redemption Chapel.

Resident Pastor

Pastor (Mrs) Olawale is the Branch Pastor of Full Redemption Chapel, Gbogbo. She is a Chartered Manager at Obafemi Awolowo University and a dedicated shepherd with rich experience in administration and ministry.
