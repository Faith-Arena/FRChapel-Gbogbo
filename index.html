About Us

Full Redemption Chapel, Gbogbo (Faith Arena) is a vibrant branch of the Jesus is Lord Foundation International. We are committed to building a Christ-centered community, reaching souls, and nurturing believers through sound teaching and spiritual growth.

About General Overseer

Rev. Dr. Francis Olonade is the General Overseer of Jesus is Lord Foundation International. Called in 1980, he is a passionate evangelist and teacher leading a global gospel mission.

Rev. Dr. Grace Olonade serves as Vice President and Senior Pastor, known for her dynamic teaching and leadership alongside her husband.

About Resident Pastor

Pastor (Mrs) Olawale is the Branch Pastor of Full Redemption Chapel, Gbogbo. She is also the Principal Assistant Registrar at Obafemi Awolowo University. Her dedication to ministry and excellence in administration helps guide the church spiritually and structurally.
